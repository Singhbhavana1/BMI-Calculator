BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple, widely-used tool to assess whether an individual's weight is proportionate to their height. It is calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters. The resulting number helps classify weight categories into ranges, such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity.

BMI is a useful screening measure to identify potential weight-related health risks but does not directly measure body fat or health. For a more comprehensive assessment, consider additional factors like body composition, muscle mass, and overall health.

BMI Formula:

BMI = weight (kg) / height (m)^2

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
  • Obesity: BMI ≥ 30
